#Maa Sakhi AI: A Voice-First Health Companion

## 1. Executive Summary
[cite_start]Maa Sakhi aims to reduce maternal and infant mortality in underserved Indian communities by providing a "trusted voice" that delivers culturally sensitive, AI-driven health support[cite: 158]. [cite_start]This voice-first multilingual AI companion supports pregnant women, new mothers, and their families[cite: 2].

---

## 2. The Problem Statement
[cite_start]In India, thousands of women face preventable risks during pregnancy, childbirth, and early motherhood because reliable and understandable health information does not reach them[cite: 4].
* [cite_start]**Information Barriers:** Medical instructions are often complex, text-heavy, and in unfamiliar languages[cite: 7].
* [cite_start]**Low Literacy & Trust:** Many women in underserved communities face language barriers and a lack of trust in digital platforms[cite: 10, 12, 15].
* [cite_start]**Overburdened Infrastructure:** Access to doctors is limited and ASHA workers are frequently overburdened[cite: 7].
* [cite_start]**Social Influence:** Family decisions are often influenced by elders who may lack accurate information or rely on myths[cite: 8].

---

## 3. Product Features & User Journey
[cite_start]Maa Sakhi AI aims to reduce risk, not just add convenience, through the following functional requirements[cite: 154]:
* [cite_start]**Voice-First Conversational Interface:** Users can ask questions via voice and receive audio responses in regional languages to accommodate low literacy[cite: 163, 164, 165].
* [cite_start]**Triage & Danger Sign Detection:** The AI identifies "danger signs" such as bleeding or fever and provides immediate escalation advice[cite: 166, 167].
* [cite_start]**Proactive Engagement:** Automated outbound calls and messages deliver stage-specific nutrition and health tips[cite: 170].
* [cite_start]**Family Education Mode:** Specifically designed content for husbands and elders to reduce "myth-based" decision-making[cite: 171, 172].
* [cite_start]**ASHA Worker Portal:** A dashboard for ASHA workers to track high-risk flags in their assigned areas[cite: 173, 174].

## 5. Success Criteria (KPIs)
* [cite_start]**North Star Metric (NSM):** Percentage of women who receive proactive, actionable guidance before a clinically defined complication window[cite: 144].
* [cite_start]**Adoption:** Number of registered mothers and ASHA worker deployments[cite: 180].
* [cite_start]**Safety:** 100% of detected "danger signs" result in a recommendation for medical intervention[cite: 181].
* [cite_start]**Retention:** Weekly active listener rate to ensure the "companion" aspect is maintained[cite: 182].
