# Maa Sakhi AI: A Voice-First Multilingual Health Companion 🌸

**Maa Sakhi AI** is a voice-first, AI-driven health companion designed to reduce maternal and infant mortality in underserved Indian communities [1, 2]. It serves as a "trusted voice" that delivers culturally sensitive, actionable health support to pregnant women, new mothers (up to 2 years), and their families [1, 2].

## 🛑 The Problem
In India, thousands of women face preventable risks during pregnancy and early motherhood because reliable health information is often inaccessible [1]. 
*   **Root Causes:** Low literacy, significant language barriers, poor connectivity, and a lack of trust in digital platforms [4].
*   **The Gap:** Medical instructions are often too complex or text-heavy, leading users to rely on verbal advice mixed with myths and fear [1, 4].
*   **The Result:** Danger signs are frequently ignored, nutrition is inadequate, and medical help is sought too late [4].

## 💡 Solution Overview
Maa Sakhi bridges the gap between medical knowledge and real-life decision-making by turning reliable health data into effective daily guidance [1, 4].
*   **Voice-First Interface:** Users ask questions via voice and receive audio responses in regional dialects to overcome literacy barriers [1, 5].
*   **Triage & Escalation:** The AI identifies "danger signs" (e.g., bleeding, fever) and provides immediate escalation advice [1, 5].
*   **Proactive Engagement:** Delivers weekly personalized voice nudges and stage-specific health tips [1, 5, 6].
*   **Family Education Mode:** Specifically designed content for husbands and elders to reduce myth-based decision-making [6].
*   **ASHA Worker Support:** A dedicated portal for health workers to track high-risk flags and access clinical references [6].

## 👥 User Personas
The product caters to a diverse ecosystem of stakeholders involved in maternal care:
*   **Sunita (Primary):** A rural first-time mother who relies on elder advice and often forgets doctor instructions [7, 8].
*   **Farida (Primary):** An urban migrant worker who lacks time for hospital visits and trusts WhatsApp misinformation [7, 8].
*   **Sana (Secondary):** An overburdened ASHA worker managing 30 visits/day seeking to improve counseling accuracy [7].
*   **Ramesh (Secondary):** A supportive husband with limited knowledge of maternal health protocols [7, 8].

## 📊 Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **North Star Metric:** The percentage of women who receive proactive, actionable guidance before a clinically defined complication window [9].
*   **Supporting Metrics:** Weekly active listeners, danger sign escalations, ANC/immunization adherence, and ASHA productivity improvement [9, 10].

## 💰 Business & Go-To-Market (GTM)
*   **Revenue Models:** Primary revenue is driven by B2G and B2B2C through government contracts (NHM budgets), NGO subscriptions, and CSR-funded deployments [3].
*   **Demand Forecasting:** Scaling strategy utilizes pregnancy registration data, ASHA worker counts, and district-wise birth rates [9].
*   **Value Proposition:** For governments, it offers a scalable, low-cost way to improve outcomes without increasing the physical workforce [3].

## 🗺 Future Roadmap
*   **v1.1:** Integration with government health IDs such as U-WIN and PMSMA [10].
*   **v1.2:** Implementation of teleconsultation features for identified high-risk cases [10].
*   **v1.3:** Introduction of premium localized diet plans and exercise subscription models [10].
